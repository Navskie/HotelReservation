LUNA PRIME

We believe that your stay should be comfortable and convenient. That's why we have designed our hotel with modern and stylish rooms that are equipped with all the necessary amenities to make your stay as pleasant as possible.

Our rooms feature air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, mini-kitchen, and water heaters. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, our accommodations provide the perfect setting to relax and unwind.

Located in the heart of Olongapo City, Luna Prime Hub offers easy access to nearby destinations such as the Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Ocean Adventure, and the Inflatable Island. Our strategic location makes it easy for guests to explore the local area and experience all that Olongapo City has to offer.

At Luna Prime Hub, we understand that affordability is important to our guests. That's why we offer affordable rates to ensure that every traveler can experience the best accommodation without breaking the bank.

So come and experience comfort and convenience at its finest at Luna Prime Hub in Olongapo City. We look forward to welcoming you and making your stay a memorable one.
